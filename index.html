How to make hot chocolate!!!

A good cup of hot chocolate can warm you from the inside out and put a messy smile on your face no matter how miserable your day was. So it's of the utmost importance that your learn this essential life skill!

ingredients

  • Cocoa powder
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Marshmallows (optional)
  • PRACTICE!

preparation

  1. Take 1/3 tablespoon of cocoa powder and empty it into your favourite mug.
  2. Add 1/2 a tablespoon of sugar.
  3. Now add around 2 tea spoons of cold milk and stir in to form a chocolaty paste. This will stop the cocoa powder swimming around the milk in undisolvable clumps.
  4. Heat 3/4 of a mug of milk in another cup in a microwave at 750 W for 2 minutes.
  5. Next, carefully pour the heated milk onto your paste and gently mix.
  6. Finally, add some marshmallows on top and watch as they slowly melt in pure bliss.
  7. Sit back and enjoy!
